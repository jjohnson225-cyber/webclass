Why He Should Be in the Rock and Roll Hall of Fame!

His music has been released for over 25 years, as the Rock and Roll Hall of Fame requires.

Not only that, his influence on the rock genre is undeniable.
The way he used his voice, the screams and shriek, arguably pioneered modern day Scream-O.
